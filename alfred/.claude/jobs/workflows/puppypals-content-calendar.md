# Puppy Pals Utah — Content Calendar

Two posts per week. The content-writer job reads this file each run to determine the next topic.

## How It Works

**Monday posts** (affiliate, 800-1200 words): Alternate between Care Tips and Product Roundups.
**Friday posts** (no affiliate links, 300-600 words): Short, casual, personal content.

- Each topic has a status: `pending`, `published`, `skipped`
- The job picks the first `pending` topic from the appropriate queue
- After publishing, update status to `published` with the post URL and date

## Notes

- Monday + Friday = 104 posts/year (52 affiliate, 52 non-affiliate)
- Monday posts alternate care tips / product roundups (800-1200 words, max 3 affiliate links)
- Friday posts are short and personal (300-600 words, zero affiliate links)
- This mix keeps the site active without feeling like every post is selling something
- Topics are ordered by what new puppy owners search for first (crate training, grooming, potty training) to front-load SEO value
- Seasonal topics (summer safety, winter care, holiday guide) should be swapped in when timely
- If a topic is skipped (bad product search, duplicate exists), mark `skipped` and move to the next
