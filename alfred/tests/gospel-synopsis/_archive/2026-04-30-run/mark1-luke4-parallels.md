## Notes

- Mark 1:12–13 is a compressed two-verse summary of the temptation; Luke 4:1–13 provides the full threefold dialogue.
- Mark places the Call of the First Disciples (1:16–20) between the Ministry Summary and the Capernaum synagogue episode; Luke 4 omits this sequence, moving directly from the Ministry Summary to the synagogue.
- The Rejection at Nazareth (Luke 4:16–30) has a loose parallel in Mark 6:1–6 (not Mark 1).
